# Record Observations and Collect Feedback

## 1. Recorded Observations (Quantitative Data)

Based on the exported logs from the prototype's User Testing Console during the 5 testing sessions:

*   **Scenario 1 (Activity Navigation):** 
    *   Completion Rate: 100% (5/5 users)
    *   Average Time to Complete: 2.4 seconds
    *   *Observation:* Users easily identified and used the bottom navigation bar.
*   **Scenario 2 (Quick Logging):** 
    *   Completion Rate: 100% (5/5 users)
    *   Average Time to Complete: 1.8 seconds
    *   *Observation:* Users gravitated immediately toward the "Quick Actions" grid on the home screen. No hesitation observed.
*   **Scenario 3 (Profile Settings):** 
    *   Completion Rate: 100% (5/5 users)
    *   Average Time to Complete: 3.1 seconds
    *   *Observation:* Two users initially clicked on their profile picture on the top-right of the home screen instead of the bottom navigation 'Profile' tab, causing a slight 1-2 second delay.

## 2. Collected Feedback (Qualitative Data)

Feedback gathered from post-test interviews and the `feedback.md` survey:

### Navigation (Average Rating: 4.8/5)
- *"The bottom navigation is standard and easy to use. I never felt lost."*
- *"I tried to swipe left to get to the Activity screen out of habit, but I had to tap the bottom bar instead. It would be cool if I could swipe."* - Emily

### Visual Design & Aesthetics (Average Rating: 4.9/5)
- *"The dark mode and glass UI look incredibly premium and calming. It feels like an expensive app."* - Sarah
- *"Animations are smooth and don't feel laggy at all."*

### Interaction & Functionality
- *"The animations on the quick action buttons are great, they actually feel like they're being pressed down."*
- *"The 'Action recorded!' popup is a bit vague. When I clicked water, I wasn't sure if it recorded water or something else. It should be more specific."* - James
- *"I tried tapping the bars on the Activity chart to see my exact calories, but nothing happened."* - Michael
