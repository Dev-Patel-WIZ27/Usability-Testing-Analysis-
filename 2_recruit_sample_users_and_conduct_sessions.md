# Recruit Sample Users and Conduct Sessions

---

## 2. Session Conduct Details

### Pre-Session:
- Users were given a brief introduction to the Zenith Wellness app concept.
- They were instructed to use the "think-aloud" protocol (narrating their thoughts as they click).
- Participants accessed the web-based prototype (`index.html`) on their own desktop web browsers.

### During Session:
- Sessions were conducted remotely via video conferencing with screen sharing enabled.
- The moderator read the 3 predefined scenarios out loud.
- The moderator observed facial expressions, hesitations, and mouse movements without interfering or providing hints.
- The prototype's built-in "User Testing Console" automatically logged exact click events, UI interactions, and screen transition times.

### Post-Session:
- Participants completed a short feedback questionnaire (`feedback.md`) regarding visual design, navigation, and overall satisfaction.
- The interaction logs were exported to a text file for quantitative analysis.
